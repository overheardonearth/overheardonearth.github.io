--- layout: post title: The Road Home comments: true date: 2019-12-07 --- I observed some time ago that a number of people name In the Mood for Love as their favorite film. I always found this perplexing. I'd found the film interesting but heavy-handed, the artistry of the film disproprotionate to its content.

More recently I realized a theory, a reason for the film's popularity: There is no alternative. Search for top ten lists of romance films, look for unthemed romances with a tragic slant, and the sparsity becomes clear.

Just now I watched The Road Home (1999) and not having written in a while, and with some lingering sentiment, I came to recommend it. I must be getting old; simple, nearly plotless stories quickly find their way onto my personal list.
